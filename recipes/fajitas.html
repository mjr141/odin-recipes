Pepper Fajitas with Refried Beans

Description

Simple and tasty vegetarian fajita dish with homemade refried beans and citrus soured cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 1 red onion
  • 3 tbsp fresh coriander
  • 1 tin mixed beans
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  • 1 tbsp tomato purée
  • 1 lime
  • 150ml soured cream
  • 4 wholemeal tortillas
  • 40g rocket leaves
